About RCCG Christ Chapel Barrie

A Place of Worship, Love, and Transformation.

At RCCG Christ Chapel Barrie, we are committed to spreading the message of faith, hope, and love through worship, fellowship, and community impact. As a family of believers, we provide a welcoming space where lives are transformed, destinies are shaped, and people experience the power of God in a personal way.

Our church stands on the foundation of prayer, evangelism, discipleship, and service, reaching out to our local community and beyond. Whether you’re seeking spiritual growth, a supportive faith family, or opportunities to serve, RCCG Christ Chapel Barrie is a place you can call home.
Join us for our Sunday services, Bible study sessions, and special programs as we grow together in Christ.

Our Mission

Spreading the Gospel

Our outreach is rooted in the belief that the message of Christ transforms lives. We actively share the Gospel through community programs, worship events, and personal testimonies, inviting everyone to experience the hope and salvation found in Jesus.

Spiritual Shelter & Care

Providing a refuge of prayer, counseling, and support for all. Through pastoral care and small groups, we walk with individuals through life’s storms, helping them thrive in God’s promises.

Nurturing Young Believers

Guiding children and youth to discover Christ’s love, ensuring they grow in faith, wisdom, and purpose. We invest in the next generation through Sunday School, youth programs, and family discipleship.

Spreading God’s Light

Bringing hope and peace to our community through outreach, prayer, and Christ-centered relationships. We strive to reflect God’s love in Barrie and beyond, fostering unity and spiritual renewal.

Growing in Faith & Knowledge

Spiritual growth is a continuous journey. We provide enriching Bible studies, prayer meetings, and educational seminars to deepen our understanding of Scripture. By fostering an environment of learning and reflection, we empower each member to grow in both faith and wisdom.

Building a Joyful Community

Community is more than a gathering—it’s a family united in Christ. Our fellowship events, small groups, and communal worship services create bonds of love and support that extend beyond the walls of our church. We celebrate life together, sharing in joys, challenges, and triumphs as one united body.
